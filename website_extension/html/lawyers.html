ButtlerBot Jobs -- Lawyer Information

Lawyers and Ranks

Determining how to portray the lawyer jobs correctly has been a rather difficult journey for me as I do not have much expertise in this area of life, however, after some brainstorming, I have made a cliff notes version. Please send me your thoughts on where I can more accurately portray the following jobs. Thanks -Mekasu

Managing Partners -- Requires Prestige Level 50+

Managing Partners are paid $15,000 per case closed. As a managing partner you are responsible for the firm as a whole. This includes but is not limited:
--Present at each case for each lawyer assigned to you.
--Ensuring correct legal proceedures are being followed.
--Reviewing financial, laboral, and quotation reports from company officers.
--Action Agreements are made as whole not as one person.
**At any time that a decision is to be made from weekly reports and all managing partners are not present, a recess is to be called until all managing partners can be present**

Financial Officer -- Requires Prestige Level 30+

The Financial Offer tracks down information from the various cases closed throughout the week and sends them to the managing partners inbox. Much like the other jobs within ButtlerBot City, the jobs are based on a scavenger hunt style-based game. Financial Officers are paid $5,000 per case closed.

Labor Officer -- Requires Prestige Level 30+

The Labor Officers' job is much like the Financial Officers job as it's a a chase through the channels as well. Labor Officers are also paid $5,000 per case closed.

Quota Officer -- Requires Prestige Level 30+

The Quota Officer produces reports as well that are based off the number of jobs performed within the week that is compared to "last years" numbers to see if the company is losing out on or gaining customers. Quota Officers are also paid $5,000 per case closed.

Lawyers -- Requires Prestige Level 20+

Lawyers are practically the grunts of the business. They're paid based off how long it takes to complete each case they are assigned to. The algorithm for this job has not been created yet thus rendering these jobs unavailable for the time being.

Para-Legals -- No Level Requirement

Para-legals are paid 1.5% of the Lawyers pay per para-legal hired to assist in each case. The more para-legals hired, the less the lawyer gets paid so be smart in your team!

Benefits

401K 401K is a percentage decided by the Managing Partners and is taken out of everyones pay.
