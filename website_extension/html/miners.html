ButtlerBot Jobs -- Miner Information

Miners

Miners within ButtlerBot City are those who dig the resources needed to print money for the city. Each city has to have miners as there is no way to send money between cities. This is to prevent nations from becoming too large in quantity and prevents capital wars. Your city must survive on its' own! mwahaha may the odds be ever in your favor!!! >:-D

Benefits

401K
A percentage decided by the Owner of the company and is taken out of everyones pay.

Pay

Miners are paid per dig they are hired for. The digs are setup in a bidding mannor in which your supervisor must be on top of their game at the bidding wars each week. For Example: If your supervisor wins a bid that pays $45,000 and he hires 15 guys to work the job, then we just follow the math:
owner = receives $5,000 flat
finance officer = receives $2,000 flat
quotas officer = receives $2,000 flat
labor office = receives $2,000 flat
supervisor = receives $1,500 flat
this gives us our remaining balance then the hired hands are paid by taking the remaining balance and dividing it by the number of hired hands leaving the gross pay per hired hand. Take that remaining balance and divide by the number of hired hands.
From there, the owner has decided the percentage that is taken from your gross pay and that is then deducted from your gross giving you your net so yo put this into a math equation. . .

$45,000 (pay from job)
-$5,000 (owners cut)
-$2,000 (finance officers cut)
-$2,000 (labor officers cut)
-$2,000 (quota officers cut)
------------
$34,000 (balance 1)
-$1,500 (supervisors cut)
------------
$32,500 (remaining balance)
(multiply by number of hired hands)
------------
(gross pay per hired hand)
(multiply by 401k percentage set by owner)
------------
(net pay direct deposited into each hired hands bank account)

Skill Sets

Company Owner -- Requires Prestige Level 50+ Receives reports from the companies financial, labor, and quota supervisors. These reports are generated based off the performance of the company versus the payouts to each employee on pay days. The owner of the company can then decide whether to change the percentage of 401K taken from the employees to increase it, but they cannot decrease it. This is to create a more challenging work environment and to prevent owners from becoming corrupt and keeping the left over money for themselves. The owner receives 3%

Financial Officer -- Requires Prestige Level 30+

Generates financial reports which shows all money incoming and all money outgoing for various areas of the company and as a whole. These reports are generated off commands that the member with this job title executes and are therein set on cool down timers which are fake loading timers that would "virtually" mimik real life report generation.

Labor Officer -- Requires Prestige level 30+

Generates labor reports which shows all hired hand hours that have been spent versus their pay rates and determines whether each hired hand, supervisor, and officer are being paid a fair wage for the work load of the job their doing.

Supervisor -- Requires Prestige Level 20+

Bids weekly on various paying jobs. The Supervisor is not bidding against other cities but rather is bidding against an algorithm that randomly decides which bids are available and gives clues as to where the supervisor can find the bids hidden within the discord channel, and what they needs to do in order to find out whether they can win the bid or fail. Think of it as one massive scavenger hunt style-based game. There will be random instructions places in various parts of the discord server and you have to find them, and follow them in order to get the next clue.

Hired Hand -- No Level Requirement

Are hired to assist with various bids. Only one Hired Hand can work One Bid at a time. This is to prevent confusion, and taking advantage of the system. The more hands that are hired to participate within the hunt, the less the pay so be smart about your crew supervisors!
