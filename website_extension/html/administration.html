About Gother:

Gother came about from the original idea of ButtlerBot. The only difference is the name change.
ButtlerBot come about one day while I was hanging out with a dear friend of mine who was working on some code back around fall of 2020. I had been interested in coding before, and had tried to many fails, although I was determined I was going to not fail this time, so I set off on an adventure.
My friend told me that the hardest part of being a programmer is being able to create something no one else has. That was my mission! I wanted to create something no one else had, so after much debate with my friend, I had decided I wanted to build a discord bot, however, the issue is there is just about every type of discord bot already created.
so what was I going to create that others hadn't? Well after almost a year of writing code, trashing it, writing more, scribbling in notebooks and ripping the pages out in frustration I just figured I would make a bot that does practically everything!

General Commands:

    whois
  • Displays an embed of various members information. As Gother grows, so will the information that is displayed in this command. This command shows the items that you put in for your profile, as well as, items that discord makes available like when your account was created, and when you joined the discord server.
