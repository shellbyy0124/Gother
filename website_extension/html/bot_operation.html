ButtlerBot Process and Command Information

Hi There! Below you will find information regarding Buttlerbot and how he acts throughout his files, what they do, and why.

  • Task Loops
  • On Guild Join
    • When ButtlerBot first joins your discord server, the first thing he does is get you squared away within his database. From recording your member count to setting your members up with an empty, but brand new bank account. A way to build a profile to display to their friends that shows accomplishments and such. Jobs that will be setup that also pay the employees every Friday given that the guild owner opts in for the city extension. [See Jobs]
  • On Guild Leave
    • When ButtlerBot is no longer a member of you server, your information is theoretically frozen. What I mean is that since ButtlerBot is a self-hosted bot, the database, and such will be stored locally on whatever hosting service you're using, or on your home pc. In the event that you bring ButtlerBot back into your server, your information will be updated in the on guild join function.
  • On Member Join
    • When a member joins the guild, ButtlerBot is setup with a function that checks if the member already exists within the database. If they don't, then it writes them to the database with fresh, but empty, information such as their guild id, member id, zero warnings, zero mutes (which are tallied when a staff member executes the warn/mute command), a $0 bank balance, and other items.
  • On Member Leave
    • When a member leaves the discord, instead of going through and trying to save all of their data, ButtlerBot will remove them completely. In the event that a user needs to be dismissed from the guild, but you do not want to completely erase their saved data so that they can return at a later date, then please use the !mute command with the time in seconds of how long they need to be dismissed from the guild.
